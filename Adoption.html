Adoption

When it finally comes time to adopting a feline friend it’s important to consider whether it’s best to adopt a kitten or an adult cat this decision should be best tailored to your lifestyle. While a kitten is exciting and cute, they typically will be more time and attention to keep them well entertained. Whereas an adult cat may be better suited towards a busier lifestyle. Make sure you examine your own life carefully before deciding.

When it comes time to shop for your cat It’s best to look at your local shelters or humane society, there are many cats in need of a good home. If you still feel uncertain on the prospects of owning a cat that’s okay. Many shelters often offer an opportunity just play with cats, this could also give you a better idea of how cats interact and if they would be a good fit for you.
